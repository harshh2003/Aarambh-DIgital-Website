What is Digital Marketing?

Digital Marketing is the process of promoting products or services using the internet and online platforms like Google, Instagram, and Facebook.
👉 In simple terms:
Digital marketing means selling or promoting anything online.
Types of Digital Marketing
1. Search Engine Optimization (SEO)
SEO helps your website rank higher on Google and brings free (organic) traffic.
2. Social Media Marketing (SMM)
Promoting products or services on platforms like:
• Instagram
• Facebook
3. Pay-Per-Click Advertising (PPC)
Running paid ads on platforms like:
• Google Ads
👉 This gives fast results.
4. Marketing
Creating blogs, videos, and posts to attract and engage customers.
5. Email Marketing
Sending emails to customers to promote products and build relationships.
